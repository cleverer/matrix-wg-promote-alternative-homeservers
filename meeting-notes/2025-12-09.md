## Ideas for Policy

- Starting points:
    - https://matrixrooms.info/indexing & https://matrixrooms.info/indexing-room
    - https://servers.joinmatrix.org/operators.html
        - (Actually I used to scan https://wiki.asra.gr/en:public_servers for public servers, usually I try to figure out who the admin is, ask them to either close it if it's not intended to be open reg, and ask them if they want to join if they are intended to the general public. Not mentioned in the documentation is a 1-month waiting period to prevent throwaway servers, though the problem still persists that running a public server is just not appealing for many.)
- Have ping stats of servers
    - JoinMatrix.org starting point: https://codeberg.org/austinhuang/matrix-serverlist-react/src/branch/main/pinger.js; but things like room count may require a setup that actually imitates a homeserver
- Have Federation stats
    - Once we have concrete policy, contact [#ping:maunium.net](https://matrix.to/#/#ping:maunium.net)
- Version up to date (JoinMatrix.org also checks for this)
- Admins/Mods
    - Are they known
    - Do they regularly talk to people
    - General Profile (who is mod team, links to mastodon, blog, github whatever)
